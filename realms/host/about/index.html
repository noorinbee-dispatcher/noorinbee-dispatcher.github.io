How did we get here?

The Noorinbee Dispatch runs by the wire, sharing access to useful information in the region.

  • It offers a ground point against the zombie scroll of social media networks.
  • The focus is on Victorian postcodes 3889, 3890 and 3891 with Cann River as a centre.
  • We are open to mediate messaging between individuals and community groups; aiming to integrate automations for this purpose without the bluster and mixed agenda of social media feeds.
  • Please contact us if you would like content appraised for inclusion, or links provisioned to other sites.
